Hill Stations in India to Beat the Summer Heat

Auli, Uttarakhand

The Garhwal region of Uttarakhand is peppered with many beautiful hill stations and Auli is one of them. Located at a height of 2909 m above sea level, Auli is a popular skiing destination in India. Thanks to snow-laden slopes and multiple ski resorts, this hill station attracts nature lovers and adventure enthusiasts in droves. Auli has many places of religious and historic importance as well. The higher slopes here offer mesmerising views of some important Himalayan peaks, like Nanda Devi, Mana Parvat, Dunagiri and Kamet. Best Places to Visit in Auli: Trishul Peak, Joshimath, Chenab Lake, Hanuman Temple, Nanda Devi National Park Things to Do in Auli: Trekking to Gurson Bugyal and Kwani Bugyal, camping and birdwatching, taking a cable car ride from Joshimath to Auli Best Time to Visit: November to March How to Reach: Nearest Airport: Dehradun’s Jolly Grant International Airport (272 km) Nearest Train Station: Haridwar Railway Station (273 km

Nainital, Uttarakhand

Situated at an elevation of 2084 m above sea level, Nainital is a beautiful hill resort in Uttarakhand’s Kumaon region. Thanks to its proximity to Dehradun and Delhi, this hill station is one of the most frequented places in North India. Boasting scenic landscapes, pleasant weather and a peaceful environment, Nainital plays the perfect host to those looking to spend time amidst nature, away from the scorching heat of plains. From boating on Naini Lake to paying your respects at Naina Devi Temple, there are plenty of things to do in this quaint little hill station. Nainital has many vantage points and trekking trails as well. Best Places to Visit in Nainital: Tiffin Top, Mall Road, Kainchi Dham, Hanuman Garhi Temple, Cave Garden, Khurpa Taal, High Altitude Zoo, Kilbury Bird Sanctuary Things to Do in Nainital: Taking a stroll at Tandi Sadak, trekking, boating and horse riding, taking a ropeway ride, shopping for local souvenirs at Tibetan and Bhotia Market Best Time to Visit: March to June How to Reach: Nearest Airport: Pantnagar Airport (59 km) Nearest Train Station: Kathgodam Railway Station (26 km)

Darjeeling, West Bengal

A prized jewel in the Himalayan Range, Darjeeling is the perfect getaway for nature lovers and honeymooners. Offering stunning views of the world’s third-highest peak – Kanchenjunga, Darjeeling is a favourite among summer travellers. It is surrounded by emerald-green slopes of tea plantations and home to some beautiful botanical gardens, monasteries, parks and vantage points. Best Places to Visit in Darjeeling: Tiger Hill, Batasia Loop, Nightingale Park, Rock Garden, Singalila National Park, Peace Pagoda, Padmaja Naidu Himalayan Zoological Park, Happy Valley Tea Estate Things to Do in Darjeeling: Enjoying the toy train ride, taking a ride on the ropeway, exploring tea gardens and monasteries, trekking and river rafting Best Time to Visit: April to June How to Reach: Nearest Airport: Bagdogra International Airport (70 km) Nearest Train Station: New Jalpaiguri Junction (73 km)

Gangtok, Sikkim

There’s nothing better than spending a few days in a clean, peaceful and natural environment, away from the din of city life. And that is exactly what Gangtok offers! A small and charming hill station in Sikkim, Gangtok is known for its scenic beauty, fresh air and serene surroundings. It boasts many natural and manmade attractions, like lakes, waterfalls and Buddhist monasteries, and is a wonderful destination for a laidback vacation. Nathula Pass and Tsomgo Lake are counted among the most popular attractions here. Best Places to Visit in Gangtok: Ban Jhakri Falls Park, Jawaharlal Nehru Botanical Garden, Ganesh Tok Temple, Gangtok Ropeway, Sikkim Himalayan Zoological Park, M.G. Marg, Seven Sister Waterfall Things to Do in Gangtok: Visiting Tiger Hill for mesmerizing views, paying respects at temples and monasteries, relishing the local cuisine, trekking, mountain biking, river rafting, riding cable car rides and going on yak safari Best Time to Visit: March to June; September to October How to Reach: Nearest Airport: Pakyong Airport (27 km); Bagdogra International Airport (124 km) Nearest Train Station: New Jalpaiguri Junction (118 km)

Mahabaleshwar, Maharashtra

Surrounded by the Western Ghats and dotted with dense forests, strawberry farms, rivers and waterfalls, Mahabaleshwar is a wonderful place for a rejuvenating vacation. This hill station is located at an elevation of 1353 m and has many amazing trekking and hiking trails if you are up for it. From seeking blessings at ancient temples and trekking to Pratapgarh Fort to boating on Venna Lake and enjoying nature photography, there are many amazing things to do in Mahabaleshwar. Best Places to Visit in Mahabaleshwar: Elephant’s Head Point, Chinaman’s Falls, Dhobi Waterfall, Arthur’s Seat, Wilson Point, Mahabaleshwar Temple, KrishnaBai Mandir, Lingmala Falls Things to Do in Mahabaleshwar: Enjoying mesmerising views from different vantage points, trying delicious berries, jams and jellies at Mapro Garden, visiting Panchgani, boating, trekking and photography Best Time to Visit: March to June How to Reach: Nearest Airport: Pune International Airport (130 km) Nearest Train Station: Wathar Railway Station (64 km)
